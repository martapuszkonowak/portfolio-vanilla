About Marta Nowak

Just the highlights

I am an ambitious junior frontend developer currently contracting at 123 Done Ltd, a web development agency that creates innovative and user-friendly websites and apps for clients across various industries. I joined the company in Jan 2023, while completing multiple online courses in web development, including SheCodes Basics, SheCodes Plus, SheCodes Responsive, and SheCodes React.

Before switching to web development, I was an Italian teacher by profession, with a MA in Italian Language and Literature from UCL. I have always been passionate about languages, both human and programming, and I enjoy learning new skills and technologies. My portfolio showcases some of my latest projects, such as a responsive weather app, a weather app built in React.js, and a personal blog. You can view them at www.martanowak.co.uk. My goal is to keep expanding my coding knowledge and to create engaging and accessible web solutions that meet the needs and expectations of users and clients.

My favourite technologies include:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • React
